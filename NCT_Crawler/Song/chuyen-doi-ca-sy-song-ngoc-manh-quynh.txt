                        Xin dung yeu toi doi ca si buon, hat bai tinh ca dang tieng cuoi vui, ru doi buon theo tieng dan, ru tinh sau trong noi niem, ru bang giong hat con tim. xin dung yeu toi doi ca si ngheo, toi doi hai ho song kiep noi troi, co gi de em uoc mo, co gi de em tuong nho, de tham trach toi tho o, dung noi gi em khi duong tinh ko chung loi, loi mong chang co cho toi, dung noi yeu toi khi doi em la hoa gam doi toi chim dam trong dem. Xin nguoi thuong toi dung chuot noi sau, toi ngoi ru em tron giac ngu me, hay mong that dep nghe em, may hong co thom hoa la nhu loi cau hat tinh ca,hay mong that dep nghe em, may hong co thom hoa la nhu loi cau hat tinh ca./.
