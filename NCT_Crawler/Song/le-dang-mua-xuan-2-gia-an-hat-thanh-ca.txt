                        Xin dang len nam moi da ve mot mua xuan binh an dat no hoa, xin dang len mong uoc chan thanh mung mot nam moi muon nguoi hanh phuc an khang.
 Nhu tran huong bay nguyen xin Chua xuan mang den thang nam troi qua, se khong doi cho, thi dang ve Chua khap xuan ca, mua xuan da ve kinh tien khuc ca mua xuan i a doi con.
 Xin dang len lan khoi huong tram ca doi con cung bao noi kho dau, xin dang len mong uoc chan thanh nguyen cho the gioi hoa binh bac ai noi noi.
 Xin dang len hoa trai thom lanh cua que huong va song nui co cay, xin dang len loi chuc xuan ve ta on Thien Chua gia nghiep hanh phuc cua con.
